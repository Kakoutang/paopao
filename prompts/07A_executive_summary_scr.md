WHEN_TO_USE: When the slide must frame the deck's central argument using the classic Situation-Complication-Resolution narrative structure. Pick this when (a) the audience needs context before diving into analysis, AND (b) the narrative arc matters more than individual data points, AND (c) the slide serves as a stand-alone summary of the entire deck.
DATA_REQUIRES: market_data

TITLE: "[Insight-driven action title with key number or contrarian claim framing the resolution]"

LAYOUT: Three horizontal bands stacked vertically. Pure narrative structure, no charts.

BAND 1 - SITUATION:
- Bold section label "SITUATION", left-aligned.
- 2-3 sentences establishing context: market size, current state, key facts. Specific numbers and named entities required.

BAND 2 - COMPLICATION:
- Bold section label "COMPLICATION", left-aligned.
- 2-3 sentences describing the tension, threat, or change that demands action.
- 2 key metric callout boxes inline: each with large bold number and 1-line label. These highlight the most critical data points driving the complication.

BAND 3 - RESOLUTION:
- Bold section label "RESOLUTION", left-aligned.
- 1-2 sentences framing the proposed response.
- 3-4 action cards in a horizontal row: each card with header bar and 2-3 bullet action items.

BOTTOM: "Key takeaway: [cross-element synthesis linking the situation's context to the complication's urgency and the resolution's recommended path]"

Source: [Real report names with year, semicolon-separated]
