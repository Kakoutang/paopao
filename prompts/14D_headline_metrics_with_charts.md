WHEN_TO_USE: When a performance dashboard must combine at-a-glance summary numbers with the trend analysis behind them. Pick this when (a) 4-5 headline KPIs need to be visible as a top-line row, AND (b) two trend charts explain the drivers behind those headlines, AND (c) a connecting commentary line ties metrics to charts in one sentence.
DATA_REQUIRES: kpi_metrics

TITLE: "[Insight-driven action title naming the dominant trend across the headline metrics and its business consequence]"

LAYOUT: Three horizontal tiers stacked vertically: metric cards top, charts middle, commentary bottom.

TOP ROW - HEADLINE METRIC CARDS:
- 4-5 cards in a horizontal row, equal width. with border.
- Each card: large bold metric number at top, KPI label below.
- Delta badge showing period-over-period change (e.g., "+8.2% YoY"). Positive, negative.

MIDDLE AREA - TREND CHARTS:
- Two charts side by side, each.
- Left chart: line or column chart showing the primary metric trend over 5-8 periods. Bars or line, data labels on endpoints.
- Right chart: different chart type (bar, area, or stacked column) showing a secondary or breakdown metric. Elements.
- Below chart: 1-2 sentence interpretation explaining the primary insight and its strategic implication.
- Both charts: axis labels, grid lines, chart title bold above each.

BOTTOM STRIP - COMMENTARY:
- Background band spanning full width.
- 1-2 sentences connecting the headline metrics to the chart trends, naming the causal link and the recommended response. Bold the key driver name.

BOTTOM: "Key takeaway: [cross-element synthesis bridging the headline numbers to the chart-revealed trend and the single priority action]"
